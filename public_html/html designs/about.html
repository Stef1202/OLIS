About City College of Tagaytay


The City College of Tagaytay in Tagaytay City is an institution of higher learning, a Local College, which was established by virtue of City Ordinance 2002-229.

In this compendium, the name of the Institution will be CITY COLLEGE OF TAGAYTAY or CCT or simply COLLEGE.

The dream of the then Mayor of Tagaytay, Hon. Francis N. Tolentino,to provide affordable quality tertiary education came into reality through the passage of City Ordinance No. 2002-229, authored by then Councilor Celso P. De Castro, thatestablished the City College of Tagaytayon September 30, 2002. The College is principally supported by local government funds.


About the Library


The library is an indispensable source of information that supports the college’s objectives and programs. The CCT has two licensed librarians, namely Mrs Leonita C. Costa and Mrs. Dormiluna E. Veluz. The current collection in the library comprise of 3,815 volumes of books.

The library is now undergoing improvement by accessing additional books from donors, widened reading space for students, physical arrangement of existing shelves and tables but still needs to procure additional tables and chairs.

The librarians are still in the process of encoding and cataloguing the books to be arranged at the card catalog for students to easily find the needed reference materials.
